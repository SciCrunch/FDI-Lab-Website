dkNET

NIDDK Information Network

About

dkNET functioned as "a centralized portal for discovery and information about research resources — data, reagents, organisms, tools available to researchers" supported by NIDDK. The initiative built upon Research Resource Identifiers (RRIDs) to advance scientific reproducibility and facilitate FAIR data practices.

FDI Lab Deliverables

  • dkNET 3.0 (Resource Reports, Discovery Portal, Reproducibility Reports, Hypothesis Centers)
  • Data management services
  • Community outreach initiatives

Selected Publications
