FSCI

FORCE11 Scholarly Communication Institute

About

FSCI is "a week-long school of intensive coursework, group activities and hands-on training around trends in scholarly communication and open research." The program brings together researchers, librarians, publishers, and technologists to advance open science and scholarly communication practices.
