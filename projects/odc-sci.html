ODC-SCI

Open Data Commons for Spinal Cord Injury

About

The project aims to establish "a digital infrastructure to democratize SCI data science" by creating a centralized database for spinal cord injury research. It aggregates preclinical data across multiple centers and species, enabling researchers to access existing datasets, contribute new data, and utilize analytical tools for discovery.

FDI Lab Deliverables

  • Open-access portal supporting data upload and publication of preclinical spinal cord research findings
