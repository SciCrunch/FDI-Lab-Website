BICCN

Brain Initiative Cell Census Network

About

BICCN aimed to "provide researchers and the public with a comprehensive reference of the diverse cell types in human, mouse, and marmoset brain." A collaborative network of integrated centers, data archives, and the Brain Cell Data Center worked together to make this cellular data accessible to the research community.

FDI Lab Deliverables

  • Ontology development for neuronal cell type classification
  • Standards and best practices documentation
  • Data analysis tools
  • Community engagement efforts
