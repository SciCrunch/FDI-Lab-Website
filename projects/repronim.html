ReproNim

Center for Reproducible Neuroimaging Computation (CRNC)

About

ReproNim focuses on advancing reproducible neuroimaging research through two main initiatives: developing accessible computational services that integrate into existing workflows, and educating the neuroimaging community about reproducibility standards. The initiative addresses "the 'last mile' implementations of core re-executability tools to increase adoption of standards and best practices."

FDI Lab Deliverables

  • Terminology services
  • Common Data Elements alignment services
  • Data discovery services

Selected Publication
