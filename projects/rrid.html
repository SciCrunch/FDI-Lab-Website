RRID

Research Resource IDentification Initiative

About

RRID supports biomedical publishing standards by enabling researchers to identify and track key resources used in scientific studies. The initiative requires authors to authenticate biological resources — including antibodies, model organisms, and research tools — by assigning stable unique identifiers called RRIDs. These identifiers appear in over 800 biomedical journals, allowing readers to identify exact resources and locate related publications.
