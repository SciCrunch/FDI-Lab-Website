CINERGI

Community Inventory of EarthCube Resources for Geosciences Interoperability

About

CINERGI compiled a comprehensive inventory of geoscience information across domains, ensuring resources had consistent descriptions, traceable origins, and complete documentation. The project addressed the absence of a unified geoscience information inventory by developing mechanisms for consistent resource descriptions covering datasets, catalogs, vocabularies, data services, process models, and repositories.

FDI Lab Deliverables

  • Data ingest, transformation, and federation infrastructure
